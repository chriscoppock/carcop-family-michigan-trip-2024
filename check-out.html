Final Day Procedures

  • Ensure all personal belongings are packed.
  • Double-check all rooms for any forgotten items.
  • Leave a thank you note.
  • Load all luggage into the car and do a final walkthrough.

Checkout Instructions

  • Strip the beds that have been slept in and remake with clean sheets. There are clean sheets for each bed in the closet of each room. In the bunk room, new sheets are on the table.
  • Put dirty linens (sheets and towels) in the laundry baskets in the utility room. If there is time to clean and dry a load of laundry, go for it. Detergent pods and bleach are on the shelves in the utility room. If no time, no worries!
  • Clear fridge / freezer / pantry of any perishables or open snacks.
  • Take out trash from kitchen and bathrooms and put in the dumpster that is in the garage. Replace trash bag (see under sink in kitchen).
  • Vacuum floors. Vacuum is hanging on the wall in the utility room.
  • Wipe down bathroom counters / toilet … just enough to not be nasty :) Cleaning supplies under the sink in the kitchen.
  • Lock back sliding doors with the pole.
  • Lock front door with code when you leave.
  • The garage should be closed, but the door between the garage and house can remain unlocked.
  • Turn off the water. There is a valve in the closet the King bedroom downstairs. ‘Off’ is when the valve is turned at a 90 degree angle from the pipe.
