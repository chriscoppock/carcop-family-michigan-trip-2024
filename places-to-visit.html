Beach

There are two beaches you can use: South Beach and North Beach. While North Beach is closer to our house, we typically go to South Beach because it’s a little larger. Cell service is pretty bad at the beach, so plan accordingly.

It is easiest to drive the golf cart there, but if you want to take your vehicle, there is a vehicle hang tag in the top drawer to the left of the dishwasher. Please return this before you leave.

Pro tip: the back seat on the golf cart will flip over to make a ‘bed’ that you can use to more easily transport beach gear.

Sunsets from the South Beach deck should be on your ‘must do’ list.

If you want to have a fire on the beach, please ‘leave no trace’ and try to build where it seems like a fire was previously built.

Note the gate to the beach gets locked at 10pm, so if out late, don’t get a golf cart or vehicle locked in for the night.

If it is super warm you can swim at night, fingers crossed. I have never done that but Jenny and her family has lots.
