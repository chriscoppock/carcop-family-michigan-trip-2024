Arrival

Address: 147 Hillside Dr, Sawyer, MI 49125


House

  • Make yourself at home! There are linens in the closet between the two upstairs bedrooms and in the linen closet across from the downstairs bedroom.
  • Thermostat is at the top of the stairs. We’re still trying to figure out this house - the vents are not balanced very well. So the upstairs will usually be about 5-6 degrees warmer than downstairs. If you are running the A/C, be aware of the variance…the ceiling fans in bedrooms help a lot upstairs!
  • Laundry detergent pods are in the utility room if you need to wash anything.
  • Because of the beach, sand tends to get everywhere. We ask that you try your best to keep it out of the house. Please wipe off or use the hose to rinse off sandy feet before going inside.
  • Please don’t wear shoes in the house! Leave them in the entry hall or outside.
  • Water may need to be turned on. The valve is in the closet in the ‘King’ room downstairs. Turn the valve one quarter turn to turn water on. ‘Off’ is when the valve is parallel to the pipe. ‘On’ is when it is 90 degrees to the right. Some pipes around the house may vibrate a bit as they refill.
  • It’s a friendly neighborhood - most people wave to each other even if you/they don’t know who they are - feel free to reciprocate.
  • Our neighborhood has a strict rental policy. I doubt anyone will ask, but if anyone asks about who you are, you can simply say you are friends of/staying with the Ottenhoff’s at 147. You are our family/friends and not renters, so the rental regulations don’t apply.

Toys

  • Beach Chairs / Beach Gear - Feel free to use whatever is in the garage.
  • Beach Flag - There is a green flag in the garage with ‘147’ on it. You must stake this in the ground near your beach stuff so beach security knows you are a resident. Put back in the garage at the house at the end of each day.
  • Golf cart - feel free to use as needed to get around. Only drivers age 16+ please.
  • Mopeds - Please DO NOT use the mopeds in the garage.
